A Living Legend

7 facts about Cr7

  • Most Goals in Men's International Football: Ronaldo holds the record for the most goals scored in international football, surpassing 120 goals for Portugal.
  • Only Player to Win Titles in 3 Major Leagues: He has won league titles in the Premier League (Manchester United), La Liga (Real Madrid), and Serie A (Juventus).
  • Five-time Ballon d'Or Winner: Ronaldo has won the prestigious Ballon d'Or award five times (2008, 2013, 2014, 2016, 2017).
  • All-time Top Scorer in UEFA Champions League: He holds the record for the most goals scored in the Champions League, with over 140 goals.
  • Euro 2016 Winner: Ronaldo captained Portugal to their first-ever major international title at the UEFA Euro 2016.
  • First Player to Score in 5 Different World Cups: He became the only player to score in five different FIFA World Cups (2006, 2010, 2014, 2018, and 2022).
  • Philanthropy and Charity Work: Ronaldo is known for his charity work, donating millions to hospitals, cancer research, and children's causes, and regularly using his platform for good causes.
